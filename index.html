The Gearing Towards Engineering Foundation

Working Towards a Brighter Future in Education

Youth in STEM

At The Gearing Towards Engineering Foundation, we are dedicated to stepping up our efforts to promote the importance of STEM education to today's youth. Publishing and promoting "Roxy the Fox and the Tree Truss Bridge" was by no means an easy feat, but through cooperation and community empowerment we believe we can facilitate progress in sharing the children's book that makes a difference. We are always striving to perform book readings, and invite you to learn more and lend your support.

The Future

With our organization’s mission always in mind, we strive to find new strategies for helping our youth prepare for their future. We hope to establish interest accruing bonds for the youth to better prepare them financially for their college education. Our team is working each and every day to make a positive impact in every way possible. Contact us to learn more about our commitment to the community.

“Education is the greatest thing you can give to someone, and knowing our book can educate and influence is absolutely amazing"

Joseph Vitale
